## 🏗️ Real Example

```markdown
# 🚀 My Founder Manifesto

**Author:** Mahtab Ali Jamali  
**Date:** May 13, 2026  
**GitHub:** [@mahtabjk123-afk](https://github.com/mahtabjk123-afk)

---

## 🌟 My Why

I want to build tools that make developers 10x more productive, saving them hours every week so they can focus on creative problem-solving instead of repetitive tasks.

---

## 💎 Core Values

### 1. Impact
*Why it matters:* I only build things that improve people's lives meaningfully.

### 2. Authenticity
*Why it matters:* I'd rather stay true to my vision than chase trends.

### 3. Excellence
*Why it matters:* Good enough is never enough. I strive for mastery.

### 4. Learning
*Why it matters:* I grow through challenges and continuous improvement.

---

## 💪 Founder Attributes I Will Develop

1. **Resilience** - I need it because startups involve constant rejection and setbacks. I'll build it through daily reflection on challenges.

2. **Clarity** - I need it because fuzzy thinking leads to confused teams. I'll build it through weekly writing exercises.

3. **Speed** - I need it because markets move quickly. I'll build it by setting tight deadlines.

4. **Empathy** - I need it because I build FOR customers. I'll build it through weekly customer interviews.

5. **Ownership** - I need it because success is MY responsibility. I'll build it through daily accountability.

---

## 🧠 First Principles

1. **Start with the customer's problem, not the solution** - Too many founders fall in love with their idea. I will validate problems BEFORE building.

2. **Simple is better than complex** - Complexity kills execution. Every feature must justify existence. I will embrace simplicity.

3. **Done is better than perfect** - Perfectionism kills startups. I will ship early, get feedback, iterate.

---

## 🎯 My Commitment

- I commit to ONE daily action moving me closer to my goal
- I commit to learning something new every day
- I commit to failing forward
- I commit to helping others
- I commit to reviewing this weekly

---

## 📊 Success in 1 Year

✅ I've launched a working product with real users  
✅ I've learned 100+ skills  
✅ I've built a small team  
✅ I've validated assumptions  
✅ I'm earning revenue  
✅ I'm living my values daily  

---

## 💌 Letter to Day 52 Me

Dear Future Me,

You made it! This bootcamp was hard, but you pushed through.

Remember why you started. Remember the struggle. Remember the growth.

This foundation will carry you through the tough times ahead.

Now go build something amazing.

— Your Day 1 Self
```
